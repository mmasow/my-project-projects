What is tennis all about?

Tennis is a popular racket sport played on a rectangular court with a net in the middle. It can be played in singles (one player on each side) or doubles (two players on each side). The objective is to hit the ball over the net into the opponent's court in such a way that the opponent cannot return it successfully. Players score points by winning rallies, and matches are typically played in best-of-three or best-of-five sets. The sport requires a combination of physical fitness, skill, strategy, and mental toughness. Major tournaments include the Grand Slam events: the Australian Open, French Open, Wimbledon, and the US Open.
